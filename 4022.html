4022 Logotype

Branding & Typography

Based off of Oswald Bruce Cooper’s Cooper Black typeface, this logotype developed for 4022 captures the brand’s unique mixture of classic retroism and new-wave edge. Softening the bulbous terminals lends a more modern look while maintaining the timelessness of the original font. Additionally, expanding and decentering the closed counters contributes to creating a typeface that doesn’t feel too serious while not sacrificing the versatility that has made Cooper Black the popular font that it still is today.
