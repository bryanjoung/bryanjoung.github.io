Club Make~Believe

3D Modeling & Branding

Created during quarantine as a form of escapism, Club Make~Believe is a monthly series of mixes that encapsulate a diverse array of genres. Each mix centers around a core mood that is reflected in the color of the vinyl record; blue for laidback and retro, green for glitchy techno, and pink for unapologetic empowerment.
